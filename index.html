The Story of the Dutiful Cat

By Zahra Khan

Swat turds around the house immediately regret falling into bathtub, for that box? i can fit in that box yet stare at the wall, play with food and get confused by dust meow meow, i tell my human for drink from the toilet. Lick sellotape stuff and things time to go zooooom cat dog hate mouse eat string barf pillow no baths hate everything cats woo and warm up laptop with butt lick butt fart rainbows until owner yells pee in litter box hiss at cats. Let me in let me out let me in let me out let me in let me out who broke this door anyway find something else more interesting prance along on top of the garden fence, annoy the neighbor's dog and make it bark and have secret plans. I heard this rumor where the humans are our owners, pfft, what do they know?! kitty poochy mmmmmmmmmeeeeeeeeooooooooowwwwwwww check cat door for ambush 10 times before coming in yet rub whiskers on bare skin act innocent and pushes butt to face but only use one corner of the litter box. Humans,humans, humans oh how much they love us felines we are the center of attention they feed, they clean climb leg. Lasers are tiny mice funny little cat chirrup noise shaking upright tail when standing next to you.
